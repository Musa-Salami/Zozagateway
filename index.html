Our Story

Founded in 2024, Zoza Gateway Snacks started with a simple idea: everyone deserves access to high-quality, freshly made snacks without leaving their home.

What began as a small kitchen operation has grown into a full-scale snack production platform, serving thousands of happy customers daily with our handcrafted treats.

We source premium ingredients, bake fresh every day, and deliver with care. Every snack we make carries our promise of quality and taste.
